Ancient Wisdom

More than a fat —
a food with memory.

Desi ghee has been at the heart of South Asian kitchens, Ayurvedic texts, and sacred rituals for millennia. When made correctly — from A2 milk, churned by hand, slow-cooked over an open flame — it carries a nutritional density that mass-produced alternatives simply cannot replicate.

OrganicMelk is made exclusively from Sahiwal and Cholistani cow milk, sourced from small family farms across Punjab and Sindh where cows graze freely. No hormones, no concentrates — just grass, sun, and time.

Each batch is numbered and traceable. We believe transparency is the most important ingredient.

How We Make It

The Bilona way — no shortcut taken.

1
Grass-Fed Milk
Collected from Sahiwal and Cholistani cows grazing on natural pastures. Never mixed with buffalo milk.
2
Set the Curd
Milk is boiled gently and seeded with live culture. Left overnight to set into thick probiotic-rich dahi.
3
Bilona Churning
Curd is churned by hand using a wooden Bilona, in both directions, to separate the white makkhan.
4
Slow Simmer
Makkhan is cooked on a low flame until golden and fragrant. Solids settle, pure ghee is filtered and jarred.
